Discussion¶

Methodology

To compare the Goal Percentage vs Shot Distance for different type of shots (wrist shot, backhand, snap shot, slap shot, wrap around) we first divide the distance from goal into 100 bins and get the the count of total shots (shot or goal) in that bin using the matplotlib.pyplot.hist() function. Then simililarly, for each bin, we get the counts of shots that were goals. To get the goal percentage for each bin, we divide the number of goals with the total number of shots for that bin.

Next, for each shot type, we plot the goal-percentage with the shot-distance. The values of shot-distance are obtained by taking the center value of each bin {(bin start + bin end) / 2)}

Observations

We notice that different type of shots have different level of efficacy depending on the distance from the goal.

For short range [0-25] we notice, that wrist and slap shots are the most lethal. However, their efficacy drops for the distance range: [30-125] but then increases again for distances greater than 125 units.

For mid range [30-110], snap shot and backhand seem to be the most lethal type of shots.

For long range [120-200] backhand, snap shot, and slap shot seem to be the most effective type of shots
